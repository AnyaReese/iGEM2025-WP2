Education

Education is the gentlest dialogue between science and humanity. We create a microscopic world of milk solidification for children and uncover the fog of cataracts for the elderly; we sow the seeds of synthetic biology in high school and ignite the passion for innovation at university. Language is no longer a barrier, dialects and multilingualism interweave into a network, allowing the voices of marginalized groups to be heard and extending the reach of knowledge to the farthest corners. Through the fusion of interesting experiments and virtual reality, the fog of cataract mechanisms turns into a starlight of exploration in children's eyes; in the strategy of card games, the wisdom of teenagers and the mysteries of life sciences quietly meet. We firmly believe that every exclamation from an experiment and every moment of contemplation in a lecture is a step towards a brighter future - because education not only conveys knowledge, but also weaves hope.

Entrepreneurship

We always believe that the mission of business is to make technology grow for good. ClearCats ® is not only a faint light hidden in the lens, but also a gentle subversion of the traditional medical shackles. We use engineering bacteria as the pen and hydrogel as the ink to write the future poetry of non-surgical treatment. In the balance of capital and conscience, we choose to let every lens carry the promise of universal benefit; in the noise of the market, we listen to the silent call of patients. Because true innovation is not in the profit ledger, but in the clear eyes of millions of people. We know that true business for good is to turn every investment into a bright spark, illuminating those forgotten corners.

Integrated Human Practices

Technology, if not nurtured by the humanities, will eventually wither. We work alongside doctors, turning the RNF114 protein from a research paper into the key to a cure; we stand by patients, using the voice prompts in the care box to ease the anxiety of those with visual impairments. Ethical review isn’t a constraint, but a shield that protects life; field research isn’t just a task, but a way to connect with the real world. In the vast expanse of technology, we always keep our eyes on the North Star of the humanities—because the greatest inventions are always born from a deep empathy for human needs.

Inclusivity

Light should not be a luxury for a few people. When technology sheds its cold exterior, it becomes a messenger of equality. We plan to design blue packaging for elderly Uyghur patients to avoid the sorrow of white in their culture; we plan to create a 'Ramadan Mode' for Muslim users, allowing faith and treatment to coexist harmoniously. In rural Zhejiang, animated tutorials bridge the gap beyond words; in a blind school in Hangzhou, tactile feedback lights the way through the darkness. Inclusion is not charity, but the recognition that every need is unique, and every life deserves a clear world.

Sustainable Development

Sustainable development is a love letter to the Earth. In our project’s plan, we replace the chains of petroleum with hydrogels derived from corn starch; we involve the sand-fixing forests of Inner Mongolia to neutralize the carbon footprint of lens production. In our factory in Hangzhou, waste materials are reborn as medical models. We deeply understand that only with technology as our pen can we write the eternal chapter of sustainable development.

Ethical Framework

In the whirlwind of technology, ethics is the reins that we always hold tight. We invite patient representatives into hearings, allowing legal experts to scrutinize every risk; in the Ethical Practices White Paper, we turn commitment into action year after year. The use of living engineered bacteria is not a cold experiment, but a vow to share the risks with patients. We firmly believe that only on the foundation of ethics can medical innovation shine with its purest brilliance—because every trust is worth being protected in the name of life.
