What is Cataract?

A cataract is a clouding of the natural lens inside the eye, leading to decreased vision. It develops gradually and can significantly impact daily activities such as reading, driving, and recognizing faces.

Why Vision Matters

Vision is crucial for independence, learning, working, and enjoying life's moments. Early detection and treatment of cataracts can help preserve vision and maintain quality of life.
