What is Cataract?

A cataract is a clouding of the natural lens inside the eye, leading to decreased vision. It develops gradually and can significantly impact daily activities such as reading, driving, and recognizing faces.

Why do People Still Suffer?

65 million live with cataracts. Surgery saves sight but leaves billions behind. Eye drops? They wash away before they work.
